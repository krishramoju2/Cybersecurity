Best Practices & Tips

  • Be specific: The more detail you provide, the better the bot’s response.
  • Use persona and tone controls to match your audience or mood.
  • Leverage the timeline to revisit or build on past work—no need to start over.
  • Try mixing features: e.g., "Translate this, make it casual, and explain as Alan Turing."
  • For study, use cheat sheets and flashcards after any technical or factual discussion.
  • If you’re ever stuck, just ask, "What can you do?" for a refresher.
